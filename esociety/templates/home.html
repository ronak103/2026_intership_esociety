Core Features

Everything your society actually needs

From the gate to the committee room — eSociety brings every moving part together in one seamless platform.

🚪
Visitor Management
QR & OTP-based digital entry. Residents approve or deny visitors instantly. Full entry-exit audit trail for security.
🔧
Complaint Tracking
Log maintenance issues with photos, auto-assign to staff, and track resolution status from open to closed.
📅
Facility Booking
Live availability calendars for gym, pool, and hall. Book and pay online. Instant confirmation, no calls needed.
💰
Financial Management
Automate maintenance dues, generate invoices, track overdue payments, and view society expense reports.
📢
Community Board
Broadcast notices, announce events, and run polls. Keep every resident informed and engaged at all times.
🚨
Emergency Alerts
One-tap SOS alerts to all residents and guards. Instant notifications for fire, unauthorized entry, or any crisis.
